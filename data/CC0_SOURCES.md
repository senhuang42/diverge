# CC0 calibration sources

The audio under the ignored `data/cc0/` directory is used only for local perceptual
calibration. Both source pages label the sounds Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0).

- `drum_loop_120bpm.wav`: “1_120bpm.wav” by pawEdit, Freesound sound 669576.
  https://freesound.org/people/pawEdit/sounds/669576/
- `guitar_loop_156bpm_c_major.wav`: “Background Guitar Loop [156bpm] [C major]” by
  deadrobotmusic, Freesound sound 570839.
  https://freesound.org/people/deadrobotmusic/sounds/570839/

The local WAVs are converted from Freesound's public listening previews and are not committed.
